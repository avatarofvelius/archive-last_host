About Me

Tamashi no Ido

Tamashi no Ido is a novela based before my beginning work in this concept (series), Advent of Arcs and Angels. While it is not first cronologicaly I believe it's going to be a good primer for the overall story and understanding the nuances of the way the world is unique, though it can be relatable to a very near future as it is. It should range around 2,000 to 4,500 words, depending on how many elements seem fleshed out and understandable.
